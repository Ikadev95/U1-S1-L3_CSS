Focaccia with Caramelized Onions, Cramberries, and Brie

Wow your yaste buds with this festive focaccia appetizer topped with sweet caramelized onions, tart cranberries and creamy brie. It will become your favorite holiday starter!

FEATURED IN:
Fresh Cranbeery Recipes That Go Beyond Sauce

Ingredients

For the focaccia:

  • 4 Cups (500g) all-purpose flour
  • 11/2 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 11/2 teaspoons salt
  • 13/4 cups (350g) lukewarm water
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil, plus more for the pan

For the topping:

  • 3 medium yellow onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1-2 tablespoons olive oil, for brushing on top
  • 10 ounces vrie, cut into cubes (scrapes rind off with a paring knife if desired, through it's not necessary)
  • 11/2 cups fresh or frozen cranberries

Method

  1. Make the focaccia dough

    2. In a medium-sized mixing bowl, or in a stand mixer with a paddle attachment, mix together all the dough ingredients until the flour is fully absorbed. You don't need to actually knead the daugh, though I sometimes knead it a few times in the bowl when mixing by heand to incorporate the last of the flour...
